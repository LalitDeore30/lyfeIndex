Lyfe.
Hello! We are Lyfe Index, ensuring everyone's story, regardless of background, is remembered and cherished for generations .

Everyone has a history and every history is interesting. The history books are only reserved for the most important people and over time, lower and middle-class names disappear. Lyfe Index is dedicated to making sure that everyone is remembered and has a legacy. Imagine your great-great-grandchildren wanting to know who you were but there were no third-person traces and accounts. Lyfe Index fixes that. Future generations can understand your life and it is a simple way to remember your loved ones.
